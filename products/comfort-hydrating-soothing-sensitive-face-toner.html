Product Description

Suitable for sensitive skin and skin after aesthetic treatments

Benefits:

  • Restore skin barrier and soothe sensitive skin
  • Tested on allergy-prone, ultra-sensitive skin
  • Deep penetrating hydration, improve moisturizing ability, reduce water loss

Brand introduction: Founded in 2010, Winona is a functional skin care brand focusing on sensitive skin. Winona has linked domestic and foreign talents in the fields of dermatology, botany, and biology to form a Winona R&D team integrating production, education and research. Based on the theory of dermatology, Winona's research and development team screened active substances based on the rich plant resources in Yunnan, developed skin care products suitable for sensitive skin, directly hit the root of sensitive skin, and repaired the skin barrier. Winona's products adhere to: no alcohol, no fragrance, no pigment, no hormone, no allergenic preservatives, simplified ingredient system, more reassuring for sensitive skin.
